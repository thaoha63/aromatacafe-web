A Founder's Story

In the misty highlands of Vietnam, the story of Aromata Coffee & Tea unfolds, curated by its visionary founder, Thao Ha. With a heart steeped in love for her homeland, Thao embarked on a sensory expedition, embracing the whispers of coffee leaves in the breeze. Her journey, a symphony of passion and perseverance, gave birth to Aromata - a sanctuary where each cup is a voyage through Vietnam's rich coffee heritage. Thao, the maestro behind the aromatic blends, invites patrons to savor not just coffee, but a cultural odyssey blending tradition and innovation. Aromata Coffee & Tea is a living testament to Thao's dream, welcoming all to indulge in the essence of Vietnam, one sip at a time.
