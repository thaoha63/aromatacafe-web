Vietnamese Black Coffee

It is an intense and bold brew, renowned for its rich, full-bodied flavor. The coffee is carefully drip-brewed, resulting in a strong and aromatic cup.

Mint Mojito Coffee

It is a cool and invigorating beverage. Expertly crafted, it combines the boldness of high-quality coffee with the zesty kick of mint and a touch of sweetness.

Egg Coffee

It, known as "cà phê trứng". This specialty drink features a creamy and velvety egg yolk custard atop a strong and robust base of Vietnamese coffee.

Cold Brew Tonic
